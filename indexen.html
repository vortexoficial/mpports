About Us

Our history is built on experience, commitment, and results.

Founded in 2019, in the city of São Sebastião (SP), MP Port Solutions was born with the objective of offering port services with a standard of excellence. Invited by a client, we expanded our operations to the Port of Santos, where we consolidated our presence with professionalism and commitment.

With a highly qualified team and an agile management model, we guarantee safe, customized, and sustainable operations.

  • Excellence and safety in port operations.

  • Efficiency and agility in all stages.

  • Sustainable solutions for an efficient port.
