Hull Cleaning

Restore Performance and Save Fuel


Restore Your Vessel's Efficiency


Biofouling — the accumulation of barnacles, algae, and slime on the hull — is one of the biggest enemies of naval efficiency. It increases drag, which forces engines to work harder, dramatically raising fuel consumption, operating costs, and pollutant emissions.

Our hull cleaning service uses cutting-edge technology to remove all fouling safely and efficiently, without damaging the anti-fouling paint. We restore the ideal hydrodynamics to your hull, ensuring maximum performance and environmental compliance.

Technology and Safety in Operation


Our team consists of certified professional divers who use hydraulic brushing equipment that adjusts to the hull's curvature. We guarantee a deep clean, respecting environmental standards and the integrity of your vessel.


  • Hydraulic brushing equipment that does not damage the paint.


  • Team of professional divers certified for port work.


  • Complete removal of biofouling (barnacles, algae, and slime) from the entire hull.


  • Video inspection (optional) to document the before and after of the service.
